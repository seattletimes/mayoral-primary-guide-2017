Aug. 1 is the deadline to drop your ballot in the mail or in a King County Elections drop box. Find more information here, on this and other races, drop-box sites, the local voters’ pamphlet, what to do if you lose your ballot and accessible voting centers for people with disabilities.

This guide is based on answers candidates provided to a Seattle Times questionnaire last month. Responses were edited for space, spelling and clarity. Some candidates didn’t answer every question and some didn’t answer each question as directed. Three candidates didn’t submit answers.
